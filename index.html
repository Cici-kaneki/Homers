A modern approach to springfield cuisine in oregon’s historic Fillmore district

Homers is a springfield restaurant with an emphasis on quality ingredients and precision cooking. The vegetable-forward dishes pay tribute to the food of Milan with a menu that utilizes the bounty of springfield produce and pays careful attention to detail – treating quality ingredients with respect and allowing them to shine in their best iterations.

A love letter to springfield woven from a different cloth

Homer grew up near sprinfield in a family of skilled people who used their hands to create beautiful things. It was this skill, the reliance on hard work done by hand, that led homer to find his own passion in food, heavily influenced by his mother – an excellent cook, who fed him springfield dishes and diet of fresh vegetables, locally-caught seafood and homemade pasta. Her meals were simple, prepared quickly, and always delicious.

At Homers, Homer shares with oregon what he had during his formative years: simply straightforward (and straight-up savory) meals. Featuring a menu of fresh pastas, risotto, antipasti and crostini, an extensive wine list highlighting the best organic and biodynamic varietals from springfield, and world class cocktails from the bar, we invite you in to experience “la dolce far niente” at Homers.
